About Me

I'm a project manager and general tech enthusiast pursuing a career in web development. When I'm not working on a coding project you can usually find me gaming, jogging or cooking (poorly). I've been passionate about technology since I was in middle school, and I've always excelled at being 'the problem solver' during my time in project management. Coding is the manifestation of those two things combined and I strive to improve as much as I can.
